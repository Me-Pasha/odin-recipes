Poha

Description

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Poha (flattened Rice)
  • Turmeric
  • Mustard seeds
  • Salts
  • Chilly powder

Steps

  1. Wash Poha and rinse the water
  2. In a fry pan, fry Turmeric, Onion and Mustard seeds
  3. Add poha in the fry pan with salt and chilly powder
